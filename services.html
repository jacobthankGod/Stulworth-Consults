Services

Everything it takes to build the company.

From the first hypothesis to early traction, we cover the work that matters most — strategy, brand, product, and growth — without turning it into a bloated process.

01 — Strategy

Know the market before you build.

Clarify the opportunity, the positioning, and the first problem worth solving so every later decision has a spine. We help founders step back from the noise and build conviction on a clear, defensible foundation.

Market research & positioning

We dig into the category, the competition, and the customer to surface a clear, defensible position. The output is not a report — it is a sharpened thesis and a concrete path forward that every team member can repeat.

Category analysis • Competitive audit • Positioning statement

Product strategy

Before you build anything, you need to know what to build and why. We help define the first wedge, the roadmap that earns early traction, and the feedback loops that keep you on course when the signal gets noisy.

First-principles thesis • Roadmap • Prioritization frameworks

Company setup & foundations

From legal structure and cap table design to bank accounts and compliance, we guide the operational setup so the company starts on solid ground. No guesswork, no expensive re-dos later.

Legal structure • Cap table • Compliance readiness
Engagement4–8 week sprint, fully remote or on-site in Abuja
DeliverablesPositioning statement, competitive map, roadmap, operational checklist
Who it's forPre-seed and seed founders with an idea or early prototype who need conviction before committing to build
02 — Brand & Business Design

Make the company legible.

Turn the idea into a coherent company with a strong identity, narrative, and offer architecture. We build the layers of legibility so investors, customers, and hires instantly understand what you do and why it matters.

Identity & voice

A company that looks and sounds coherent earns trust faster. We build visual identities, verbal guidelines, and tone-of-voice systems that make your company legible across every touchpoint.

Visual identity • Verbal guidelines • Brand systems

Offer architecture

Most early companies have a product but no clear offer. We help package what you do into buyable outcomes — with pricing, positioning, and a structure that makes it easy for the right customers to say yes.

Offer design • Packaging strategy • Pricing models

Narrative & messaging

Your story is the shortest path to alignment. We craft the founding narrative, the investor story, and the go-to-market messaging so every audience hears one coherent version of what you are building.

Founding narrative • Investor story • GTM messaging
Engagement3–6 week sprint, includes stakeholder workshops and design reviews
DeliverablesVisual identity system, voice guide, offer deck, narrative one-pager
Who it's forFounders preparing for launch or raising who need the company to feel older and more intentional than its calendar age
03 — Product

Build the product and the rhythm.

Shape the product and the operating systems that keep the business moving after the first milestone. We work alongside your team to ship fast without sacrificing direction.

Product direction

Senior product leadership without the full-time salary. We step in to guide discovery, define the product roadmap, and keep the team building the right things — with a bias toward shipping, not over-planning.

Product discovery • Roadmap • Interim leadership

Operating systems

The teams that win are the ones that coordinate well. We install lightweight planning cycles, decision frameworks, and metrics rhythms that fit a team of four and scale to forty.

Planning cycles • Dashboards • Team rhythm

Build & delivery

When the team needs hands-on support to ship the first version, we step into the build — designing workflows, choosing tooling, and running delivery sprints alongside your team until the muscle is yours.

Sprint execution • Workflow design • Tooling setup
Engagement2–3 month embedded sprint, weekly check-ins with founder
DeliverablesProduct roadmap, operating system playbook, shipped MVP or milestone
Who it's forTeams of 2–10 who have validated the idea and need help building the first version and the rhythm to sustain it
04 — Growth

Bring it into the market, calmly.

A focused launch motion and go-to-market support, plus ongoing advisory as you scale. We help you enter the market with clarity, not noise, and build the early traction that compounds.

Launch motion

A launch is not an event — it is a sequence. We help plan the concentric circles of outreach, craft the announcement narrative, and build the runway so the launch earns real attention, not just a spike.

Launch sequence • Announcement narrative • Outreach strategy

Go-to-market

Channel by channel, message by message. We map the GTM plan: which channels earn early traction, what message works where, and how to convert early signals into repeatable pipeline.

Channel strategy • Conversion • Pipeline design

Ongoing advisory

Company building is not a project. We stay close with a retainer or on-call rhythm — available for the hard decisions, the board prep, and the moments when an outside perspective changes everything.

Board prep • Strategic counsel • Growth reviews
Engagement4-week launch sprint, then ongoing advisory retainer available
DeliverablesLaunch sequence plan, narrative assets, channel playbook, growth dashboard
Who it's forFounders within 4–8 weeks of launch who want a calm, prepared entry — not a chaotic spike
